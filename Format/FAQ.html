Q. Neat. First thing I should do is join a Social Media platform, then I can play some games.
What if I need help if I get stuck on the Social Media platform?
A. Chat with a Social Media Moderator.
These are the people who enforce the Community Constitution but also are there to assist with their own Social Media and it's channels or tabs.
Moderators cannot assist with technical issues or bugs, but can help with rules enforcement, rules questions, and how to find a Social Media function in the community.

Q. Okay that is good. How about if I have a technical issue, need access to something technical on the website or Social Media platform,
or have an idea / improvement I want to see done on the website or Social Media platform.
Who do I talk to?
A. Feel free to ask one of the IT members.
If none are available talk to a Mod as the Mods all have direct connections to IT so that an IT member can find and talk to you.
IT cannot assist with user moderation or rules enforcement, but can help with bots, platform functions, and many other tasks.

Q. Super! So how about if I have a card I want banned, unbanned, or a rule changed.
How do I make my change happen?
A. We want to see your suggestion. Send it over to the Community Proposals page found HERE
Be sure to include as many details as possible. The more details about a specific concern the better the odds it will be passed into the format.
Don't forget to include your Discord username or some kind of user handle so you have a chance to update it later and if we need to contact you back to make it viable for a vote.
If you don't sign it you run the very real risk of having the proposal denied.

Q. That sounds simple. Details details details, or else lack of results.

A. All proposals past, present, and future will be found HERE

Q. Okay. So who is in charge of the format? Who is the manager or boss?
A. No one is.
Each department is responsible to complete tasks, but there is no such thing as a manager or boss or ruler of any kind.
This is a democratic format meaning there is zero hierarchy, only responsibilities which differ in different departments.

- Elected Administrators filter proposals, push vote requirements to IT to setup
- Scribe tracks and moderates Elected Administrator events, and publish vote results on an as needed basis
- Social Media (Moderators) enforce the Community Constitution on Social Media platforms, as well as moderate bad faith members on their own platforms
- IT handles technology maintenance, host technical infrastructures, change management, and follows requests of other departments while maintaining integrity of platform security
